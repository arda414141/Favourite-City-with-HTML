Geographical Location:

London is the capital city of England and the United Kingdom. It is located in the southeast of England, along the River Thames.

History:

London has a rich history dating back to Roman times. It was originally called Londinium and has since become one of the world's most significant cities in terms of culture, finance, and politics.

Interesting Places:

  • The Tower of London - A historic castle and UNESCO World Heritage Site.


  • Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament - Iconic landmarks of London.


  • The British Museum - One of the world's largest and most important museums.


  • Buckingham Palace - The official residence of the British monarch.


  • Battersea Power Station - Battersea Power Station is a decommissioned coal-fired power station.
